Brotherhood of Steel Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

Site Name

Brotherhood of Steel Chamber

This name was selected to reflect the Brotherhood of Steel's dedication to regulating and facilitating trade within its territories. The word "Chamber" indicates the formal and organized approach to commerce.

Site Purpose

The Brotherhood of Steel Chamber of Commerce website aims to:

  • Regulate and facilitate trade, ensuring standardized practices and resource management.
  • Promote technological advancements and support local businesses through economic stability initiatives.

Scenarios

Potential questions from site visitors:

  • What events will the Chamber be holding this month to promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the Chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the economic growth in the Brotherhood's territories?

Color Schema

The color scheme for the website will reflect the Brotherhood's purpose and trademark:

Darker Green (#006400): Used for headers, navigation background, and footer background.

Dark Gray (#333): Used for body text and some backgrounds.

Lime Green (#32CD32): Used for h3 text color, links, and some accents.

Typography

The website will use the following fonts:

  • Arial: Used for body text due to its readability and wide availability.
  • Georgia: Used for headings to provide a classic and authoritative look.
