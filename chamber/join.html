Brotherhood of Steel Membership Levels and Benefits

Initiate

  • Access to basic training programs
  • Participation in entry-level missions
  • Supervised use of Brotherhood facilities

Scribe

  • Access to research and archival materials
  • Participation in technological projects
  • Mentorship from Senior Scribes
  • Opportunity to contribute to Brotherhood publications

Knight

  • Full combat training and advanced weapon access
  • Leadership roles in missions and patrols
  • Maintenance and repair duties for Brotherhood equipment
  • Eligibility for Senior Knight promotion

Paladin

  • Elite combat training and access to power armor
  • Command over squads and larger operations
  • Participation in high-stakes missions
  • Influence in strategic planning and decision-making

Elder

  • Top leadership and decision-making authority
  • Guidance over Brotherhood chapters and missions
  • Access to all Brotherhood resources and knowledge
  • Representation in inter-faction negotiations
