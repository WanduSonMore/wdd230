About Us

Nestled within the treacherous ruins of the Mojave Wasteland, the Gun Runners stand as a beacon of security, innovation, and unparalleled craftsmanship. Since the days following the Great War, the Gun Runners have dedicated themselves to the manufacture and distribution of the finest weapons and ammunition, earning a reputation that is both feared and respected across the Wasteland.

Our Mission
At Gun Runners, our mission is simple: to provide the highest quality arms and armament to those who need them most. Whether you are a lone wanderer seeking protection against the perils of the Wasteland or a faction leader looking to equip your troops, we ensure that every firearm and piece of ammunition that bears our name meets the highest standards of reliability and precision.

Our Craftsmanship
Our engineers and craftsmen are among the most skilled and knowledgeable in the entire Mojave. Each weapon is meticulously crafted using salvaged materials, precision machinery, and the expertise passed down through generations of weapon specialists. From the reliable 9mm Pistol to the devastating Lazer Rifle, every piece in our arsenal is designed to perform flawlessly in the most hostile environments.

The Gun Runners Arsenal
Our extensive catalog includes a diverse range of weapons and ammunition to suit every need. Some of our most sought-after items include:

  • Lazer Pistol: A high-energy weapon that combines compact design with lethal precision.
  • Grenade Launcher: Ideal for crowd control and taking down heavily armored opponents.
  • Machine Gun: Perfect for those who require sustained firepower in intense combat situations.
  • Alien Gun: A rare and exotic weapon with unmatched destructive capabilities.


Commitment to Our Customers
We believe in forging lasting relationships with our customers. Our knowledgeable staff is always ready to provide expert advice, ensuring you select the weapon best suited to your needs. Moreover, our commitment to quality means that we offer maintenance services to keep your weapons in optimal condition, ensuring they remain as reliable as the day you acquired them.

Location and Availability
You can find our main outlet near Freeside, just outside New Vegas. Despite the dangers that lurk in the Mojave, we have managed to establish a secure and accessible location for our customers. Additionally, our caravan routes are well-guarded, ensuring that our products reach you no matter where you are in the Wasteland.

Join the Gun Runners Legacy
In a world where danger is a constant companion, the Gun Runners offer the peace of mind that comes with superior firepower. Trust us to be your partner in survival, and experience firsthand the excellence that defines our legacy.
